About Modoc Vineyard

Our Story, Leadership, Values, and What to Expect

Our Story

Planted in fall of 2019, Modoc Vineyard is rooted in rural Northern California and centered on living out the word and works of Jesus.

Our Leadership

Senior Pastor Niki Wolter and her husband Corey planted Modoc Vineyard in August 2019 after serving at Red Bluff Vineyard. They have been part of the Vineyard movement since 2004.

Mission, Vision, Values

We are a people of the Kingdom of God who partner with the Holy Spirit, worship God, practice compassionate ministry, pursue mission, and seek reconciliation with God and creation.

What to Expect

A warm welcome with coffee and friendly faces, worship together, a biblical message, prayer, communion, and outreach opportunities.

The Wisdom to Follow. The Courage to Lead.
